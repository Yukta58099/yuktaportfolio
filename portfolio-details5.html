Analysis-Of-Geotracking-Data

This project involves the use of K-Means Clustring to find the best accommodation for students in Bangalore(or any other city of your choice) by classifying accommodation for incoming students on the basis of their prefernces on amenities, budget and proximity to location.

Project Context

  • This project teaches the daily life of a data science engineer, covering data preparation, visualization, machine learning algorithms, and presentation. It highlights the importance of food in a person's lifestyle, especially when moving into a new location. Living close to preferred outlets can save time and hassle for students and food providers. Managers of restaurants and hotels can also leverage this information to open at locations with high customer concentration, ensuring short commute times and more customers. This project is suitable for beginners and professionals, and can be applied to any location.
